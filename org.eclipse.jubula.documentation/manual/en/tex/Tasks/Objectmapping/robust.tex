\index{Changes in the AUT}
\label{omchanges}
If your \gdaut{} has changed from one version to the next, your tests may still run, depending on the nature and extent of the changes. 

\bxtipp{Please bear in mind that changing the look and feel of your \gdaut{} may lead to different technical names for the components in your \gdaut{} if you have not named the components.} 

If your test does not run, a few simple steps will update it.

If you get a \bxname{component not found} error, or the wrong component is selected during the test, remap the new component from the \gdaut{} and assign it to your component name. 

This updates this component for the whole test.
