\index{Bottom-Up Specification}
\index{Specification!Approaches!Top-Down}
\index{Specification!Approaches!Bottom-Up}
\jb{} supports both bottom-up and top-down testing. You can even use both methods in the same \gdproject{}, and often, you can easily change between the two.  

The following sections explain a little about each approach, and its advantages and disadvantages. 
The best method to choose will depend on the size and type of the \gdproject{}, the stage of testing, the \gdaut{} and your personal preferences.
