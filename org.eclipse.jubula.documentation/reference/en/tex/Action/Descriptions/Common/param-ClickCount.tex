\begin{itemize}
\item Enter the amount of clicks that should be performed on the component or item.
\item If you enter \bxshell{0}, the mouse pointer will just be moved over the specified component or item.
\item Entering \bxshell{1} (without quotes)  will single-click on the component or item.
\item Entering \bxshell{2} (without quotes) will double-click on the component or item. 
\end{itemize}
