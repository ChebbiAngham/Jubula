3.15.6 Object mapping and AUT changes

If your AUT has changed from one version to the next, your tests may still run, depending on the nature and extent of the changes.
Please bear in mind that changing the look and feel of your AUT may lead to different technical names for the components in your AUT if you have not named the components.

If your test does not run, a few simple steps will update it.

If you get a component not found error, or Jubula selects the wrong component during the test, remap the new component from the AUT and assign it to your component name.

This updates this component for the whole test.
